life beyond the classroom

Learn Spanish.
Live Bolivia.

eight ways to immerse

The full experience

These aren't extras — they're where the real learning happens. Every activity is led by a teacher or local guide, and everything runs in Spanish.

Every Tuesday · 3 hrs Bolivian Cooking Class
Food & Culture

Bolivian Cooking Class

Cook a traditional Bolivian meal from scratch in the Me Gusta café kitchen. Your teacher guides you through the whole process in Spanish — shopping vocabulary, recipe instructions, food culture. Then you eat what you made.

Every Tuesday 3 hours All levels welcome
Saturday mornings Market Morning
City & Culture

Market Morning

An early-morning guided walk through Sucre's Mercado Central with one of our teachers. You'll learn the names of every vegetable, spice, and cut of meat — and how to haggle politely.

Saturday 8am 2 hours
Mon & Thu · 2 hrs Sucre City Walk
History & Architecture

Sucre City Walk

Walk the White City with a teacher-guide who brings its colonial history to life — entirely in Spanish. From the Plaza 25 de Mayo to hidden courtyards and rooftop views.

Mon & Thursday 2 hours
Every Friday · 2 hrs Salsa & Cumbia Night
Music & Dance

Salsa & Cumbia Night

Weekly dance session with a local instructor. Your teacher translates moves and cultural context. No experience needed — just be ready to laugh and move.

Every Friday 2 hours All welcome
Wednesday evenings Cinema & Conversation
Film & Discussion

Cinema & Conversation

A Latin American film screened in the café, followed by an open discussion in Spanish. A brilliant way to hear natural speech, absorb vocabulary in context, and talk about things that matter.

Wednesday 7pm Intermediate+
Thursdays · from 6pm Conversation Evening
Speaking Practice

Conversation Evening

Informal gathering at the café with teachers, students, and local guests. No formal structure — just real conversation, great coffee, and the kind of connections that stay with you long after you've left Sucre.

Every Thursday From 6pm Free
Every Sunday · full day Tarabuco Day Trip
Excursion

Tarabuco Day Trip

A full-day Sunday excursion to Tarabuco, 1 hour from Sucre. One of Bolivia's most vibrant indigenous markets — Jalq'a and Yampara communities gather here in traditional dress. Your teacher guides you through, Spanish only.

Every Sunday Full day Transport included
By arrangement Photography Walk
Language & Art

Photography Walk

A Spanish-language photography walk through Sucre. You describe what you see, what light does to a building, what a person's expression makes you feel. Descriptive vocabulary, adjectives, storytelling — all without feeling like a lesson.

Flexible scheduling 2–3 hours Small groups
how it works

Simple to join

01

Sign up

Tell us which activities interest you when you register, or drop us a message anytime. Most activities are free or low cost for enrolled students.

02

Show up

Everything runs from the school or the café. No complex logistics — we keep it easy so the focus stays on the experience, not the planning.

03

Speak Spanish

Every activity runs entirely in Spanish. Your teacher is always with you — there to help, not to correct. It's the fastest way to build confidence outside a classroom.

Good to know

All activities are included in your study package or available for a small additional fee. Schedules may vary seasonally — ask us when you register and we'll confirm what's running during your stay.
