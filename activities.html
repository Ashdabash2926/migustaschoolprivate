scroll to wander the week→
Monday

Sucre City Walking Tour

Afternoon·2 hrs
History & Architecture

The White City with a teacher-guide who brings its colonial history to life — Plaza 25, hidden courtyards, rooftop views.

Tuesday

Bolivian Cooking Class

Afternoon·3 hrs
Food & Culture

Step into a Sucre local's kitchen, cook a traditional Bolivian meal together, then stay for board games and a proper Bolivian evening — all in Spanish, the way locals actually spend their nights.

Wednesday

Wallyball Night

Evening·1.5 hrs
Sport & Social

Volleyball meets four walls. Impossible to play without shouting in Spanish — the vocabulary sticks, fast.

Thursday

Language Exchange

Afternoon·1.5 hrs
Conversation & Connection

Bolivian locals come to the school to swap languages — they want to practise English, you want to practise Spanish. Pair up, switch every fifteen minutes, and walk away with new friends and a phone full of contacts.

Thursday

Salsa Dancing

Evening·1.5 hrs
Music & Movement

Step, turn, count to eight in Spanish — a local instructor takes you from first basic to your first full song. No partner needed, no experience required, just a willingness to laugh at yourself in another language.

Friday

Sucre Beer Tour

Evening·2–3 hrs
Drinks & Social

End the week inside one of Sucre's craft breweries — stainless tanks, hand-labelled tasting flights, and the brewer walking you through malt, fermentation and the pint in your hand, all in Spanish.

how it works

Simple to join

01

Sign up

Tell us which activities interest you when you register, or drop us a message anytime. Most activities are free or low cost for enrolled students.

02

Show up

Everything runs from the school or the café. No complex logistics — we keep it easy so the focus stays on the experience, not the planning.

03

Speak Spanish

Every activity runs entirely in Spanish. Your teacher is always with you — there to help, not to correct. It's the fastest way to build confidence outside a classroom.

Good to know

All activities are included in your study package or available for a small additional fee. Schedules may vary seasonally — ask us when you register and we'll confirm what's running during your stay.
