Option 1 — most popular

Live with a Bolivian family

Living with a Bolivian family is highly recommended. Not only do you practise more Spanish — you also learn about Bolivian culture, customs, and get to experience delicious Bolivian cuisine every single day.

All our host families are carefully selected and we maintain ongoing communication with them throughout your stay. Every family is located within a 10-minute walk of the school — so you're never far from class.

Option 2 — your own space

Student Apartment

Prefer your own space? Our student apartment is a comfortable, social base right in the heart of Sucre. You'll be surrounded by other international students — great for practising Spanish and making friends from around the world.

Breakfast is included in both options. Choose between a shared or private room depending on your preference and budget.
