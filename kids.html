how kids learn here

Learning through play

No textbooks, no grammar drills. Our kids programme uses songs, games, storytelling and hands-on projects to build real Spanish — naturally and joyfully.

🧸

Little Explorers

Ages 4–7 · 45-min sessions

Songs, puppets, colours, animals and storytelling. A gentle, playful introduction to Spanish for the youngest learners.

🗺️

Young Adventurers

Ages 8–11 · 1-hour sessions

Games, role-play, basic conversation and cultural projects. Kids build confidence speaking Spanish through fun, immersive activities.

🌎

Teen Travellers

Ages 12–15 · 1.5-hour sessions

Conversation practice, real-world scenarios and city exploration. Teens develop practical Spanish skills while discovering Sucre.

beyond the classroom

Fun for the whole family

🗝️

Treasure Hunts

Explore Sucre's streets and plazas following clues in Spanish. Kids practise reading, asking directions, and working as a team.

🍳

Cooking Class

Make simple Bolivian dishes together — learn food vocabulary, follow recipes in Spanish, and eat what you cook.

🎨

Art & Crafts

Paint, draw and create using traditional Bolivian techniques. All instructions in Spanish — colours, shapes, and materials become vocabulary lessons.

🛒

Market Mornings

Visit Sucre's central market with a teacher. Kids practise numbers, prices and polite requests while buying fruit and snacks.

📖

Story Time

Bolivian folk tales and picture books read aloud in simple Spanish. Great for listening skills and building imagination.

💃

Dance & Music

Learn traditional Bolivian dances and songs. Movement, rhythm and repetition make new words stick — and it's impossible not to smile.

for parents

The family experience

🏡

Family Accommodation

Our host families welcome children warmly. Safe neighbourhoods, home-cooked meals included, and a real Bolivian family experience for everyone.

👩‍🏫

Parent Classes

Adults study in the regular programme while kids are in theirs — same hours, same building. Everyone learns, nobody waits.

📅

Flexible Scheduling

Book by the week, combine kids and adult classes, or choose part-time options. We work around your family's plans, not the other way round.
