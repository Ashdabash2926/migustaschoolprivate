In Sucre, Bolivia

Learn Spanish in the heart of South America

Join our in-person Spanish classes and live the language every day. Sucre is one of Bolivia's most beautiful colonial cities — a perfect backdrop for language learning, cultural discovery, and making memories.

Our native Bolivian teachers bring grammar, conversation and culture into every session. Whether you're a complete beginner or polishing your fluency, we tailor every lesson to you.
