Coffee with a story

Every cup is sourced from regions with extraordinary growing conditions — from the misty valleys of Bolivia to the fields of northern Italy.

01
Yungas, Bolivia

Yungas Valley

Grown in the cloud forests of the Bolivian Andes at 1,500–2,000m. Rich volcanic soil and high altitude produce a coffee of exceptional clarity and complexity.

Dark chocolate Citrus Floral
02
Caranavi, Bolivia

Caranavi Hills

Bolivia's most celebrated coffee-growing region. Fertile foothills and an ideal microclimate produce a naturally sweet, full-bodied cup that's become a world favourite.

Brown sugar Walnut Stone fruit
03
Northern Italy

Lavazza

Italy's most iconic coffee house, roasting since 1895. For those who want that classic, robust Italian espresso — smooth, strong, and completely reliable.

Roasted nuts Caramel Classic espresso

for students & guests

A unique study space unlike any other

Me Gusta Café is open to all students as a dedicated study and relaxation space. Between classes — or after — pull up a chair, order something delicious, connect to the WiFi, and let the language sink in.

Many students find that reviewing vocabulary over a cortado, or practising conversation over lunch, is where the real learning happens. The café is more than a café — it's part of the school.

Fast WiFiReliable high-speed internet, free for all students
Power OutletsCharging points throughout the café and courtyard
