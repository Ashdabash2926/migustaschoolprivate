little tricks, big results
Memory techniques we love

Making new words stick

We teach you how to build a personal vocabulary that stays with you — using techniques drawn from memory science and our own years of experience in the classroom.

Mental Associations

Connect new Spanish words to sounds, images or stories that already mean something to you. Your brain remembers meaning far better than isolated vocabulary lists.

Flashcards with Context

Not simple translation cards — but two-sided cards with the word used in a real sentence. Context is everything. You remember how a word feels, not just what it means.

think about how children learn...

Children speak fluently without ever studying grammar.
So can you.

Children learn their first language through interaction, listening, imitation and feedback — not through memorising rules. Our methodology draws on this natural process. We give you grammar as a tool, not as the destination. Real conversation, real culture, real Spanish — from your very first lesson.
